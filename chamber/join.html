NP Membership Benefits

Our NP Membership is designed specifically for non-profit organizations seeking to connect with the business community and access networking opportunities.

Benefits Include:

  • Networking events access
  • Community directory listing
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Access to chamber forums
  • Social media promotion
  • No membership fees

Perfect For:

Charitable organizations, educational institutions, and community development organizations looking to build partnerships with the business sector.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze Membership is ideal for startups and new businesses entering the Accra market. Get the foundation you need to grow your network and visibility.

Benefits Include:

  • All NP membership benefits
  • Monthly networking breakfast events
  • Discounted event registration (20%)
  • Basic website listing with logo
  • Email updates on business opportunities
  • Access to member resources

Investment:

GHS 500 per year - Perfect entry point for new businesses

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver Membership offers comprehensive benefits for growing businesses ready to expand their reach and establish stronger community connections.

Benefits Include:

  • All Bronze membership benefits
  • Priority event registration
  • Discounted events (30%)
  • Featured website listing with description
  • Quarterly business training workshops
  • Special networking mixers (monthly)
  • Spotlight newsletter feature (once per year)
  • One free booth at annual trade show

Investment:

GHS 1,500 per year - Ideal for established growing businesses

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold Membership is our premier tier, offering maximum visibility and exclusive benefits for established enterprises committed to community leadership.

Benefits Include:

  • All Silver membership benefits
  • Premium website listing with full page profile
  • Discounted events (40%)
  • Priority event sponsorship opportunities
  • Exclusive executive roundtable events
  • Quarterly publication feature
  • Free booths at all trade shows
  • Recognition as key chamber partner
  • Custom marketing package
  • Direct access to chamber leadership

Investment:

GHS 3,000 per year - Premium membership for community leaders
